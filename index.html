Memphis Tigers Football!

History

Memphis Tigers football team represents the University of Memphis in college football in the NCAA Division I Football Bowl Subdivision.
The Tigers play in the American Athletic Conference as an all-sports member. They play home games at Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium.
The team's head coach is Ryan Silverfield.
Since their inaugural season in 1912, the Memphis Tigers have won over 450 games and appeared in 13 bowl games.
The program has also claimed eight conference championships with the most recent coming in 2019.
